CollegeMichigan

RoleDefensive End

ABOUT

Wormley was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the third round, 74th overall, in the 2017 NFL Draft. On October 8, 2017, Wormley made his NFL debut, logging 24 snaps and recording his first-career tackle against the Oakland Raiders.[18] The next week Wormley made his first-career start against the Chicago Bears, recording two tackles and his first quarterback hit.[19] He ended his rookie season appearing in 7 games with 2 starts, logging five tackles and a quarterback hit.


On March 20, 2020, Wormley was traded to the Pittsburgh Steelers along with a 2021 seventh-round pick, for the Steelers 2021 fifth-round draft pick. Wormley was placed on injured reserve on October 31, 2020, after suffering a knee injury in Week 7. He was activated on November 21, 2020.
